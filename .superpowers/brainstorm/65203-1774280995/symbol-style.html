Flow Diagram Symbol Style

Small uniform symbols with external labels — which visual treatment?

A. Minimal Dots

Filled circles for process, diamond outlines for decisions. Matches Tufte minimalism. Spine is a subtle gray line.

B. Transit / Metro

Hollow station circles on a thick spine, like a subway map. Findings terminate at colored dots. More visual weight, very scannable.

C. Hybrid Minimal

Thin spine, small dots, but findings use a vertical stem (like the manhattan transit graph). Finding title leads, badge trails. No boxes around findings — just text hanging off the stem.
